What is SEO and why is Important?

SEO is the process of improving the quality and quantity of website traffic to a website or a web page from search engines.

List of reasons why SEO is important for companies:

  • SEO Brings Free Traffic (ROI)
  • A Search Engine Optimized Site Gets More Traffic
  • SEO is a Great Long Term Marketing Strategy
  • SEO helps you beat competitors
  • SEO improves the user experience
